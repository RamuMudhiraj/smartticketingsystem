SmartTicketingSystem

A **Smart Ticketing System** website is a digital platform that allows users to purchase, manage, and validate tickets online for transportation, events, and other services. It offers features such as online booking, QR code-based validation, secure payment integration, and real-time updates, enhancing user convenience and reducing the need for physical tickets. The system supports various payment methods and ensures security through encryption and authentication. With mobile compatibility and data analytics, it provides insights into ticket sales and user behavior, making ticket management efficient, cost-effective, and user-friendly.
